If the directions your school gives you are different than those provided by the e-Decision Tree,
it could be because:

 

 

·         The information the school is basing its decision on is different from what you’ve provided, or

·         The school may not be using all of the options covered by the e-Decision Tree.

 

 

The CDPH guidance and county public health orders provide several options for isolation and quarantine.

 

 

·         The e-Decision Tree is designed to identify recommendations that provide for the earliest return to school or work, based on these options.

·         Schools are not required to make all of the options available to students or staff.

·         School leaders are permitted, based on the conditions in their community, school district and/or school, to allow use of only the more conservative (longer) options in the guidance.
